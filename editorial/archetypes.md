## Availability Risk Archetypes

### Managed-Availability Star
Example: Anthony Davis
- frequent minor issues
- plays often, but unpredictably
- downside = sudden zero

### Intermittent Shutdown Risk
Example: Joel Embiid
- clustered absences
- high ceiling, fragile floor

### Load-Managed Veteran
Example: Kawhi Leonard
- performance intact when active
- rest patterns dominate
